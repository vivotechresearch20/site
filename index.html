Clinical Research Solutions Partner

Vivotech Research Lab Pvt. Ltd. offers a comprehensive, end-to-end and tailored-made bio-equivalence and clinical trials solutions to support innovator and generic drug development programs for our national and international clientele.
We are a young, vibrant and innovative company in the field of clinical research solution services. Our leadership team with a combined experience of 100+ years have the vision to change and offer solutions to be strategic and preferred partners to our clientele.
Together, we want to build solutions that will drive the clinical research forward by smartly connecting data, tech and operational expertise to solve complex and challenging clinical trials and develop a more efficient and effective ways to ease these complex challenges.
