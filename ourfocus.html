Our Focus

Vivotech Research Lab Pvt. Ltd. is a full-service Clinical Research Organization (CRO) committed to providing end-to-end solutions for clinical research and pharmaceutical development. Our expertise spans bioequivalence studies, clinical endpoint studies, and bioanalytical testing, serving pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies worldwide. We leverage cutting-edge technology, a highly skilled team, and state-of-the-art facilities to deliver cost-effective, high-quality, and ethically driven research solutions.

At the core of our services are bioequivalence and bioavailability studies, where we specialize in evaluating pharmacokinetic and pharmacodynamic parameters, exploring drug-drug interactions, and conducting studies for different populations, including those with special health conditions. Our bioanalytical testing capabilities include method development, validation, and the use of advanced LC-MS/MS platforms for accurate and precise drug quantification.

We also excel in regulatory and medical writing, ensuring comprehensive and compliant submissions for various regulatory milestones, from IND applications to NDAs and BLAs. Our independent quality assurance team adheres to rigorous cGMP, cGLP, and cGCP standards, conducting system audits and study audits to maintain the highest levels of compliance and data integrity.

Vivotech's clinical trial services cover the entire spectrum of drug development, from Phase I to Phase IV studies. Our offerings include site feasibility analysis, protocol development, site monitoring, project management, and data management using industry-standard electronic data capture platforms. We also specialize in safety and pharmacovigilance, ensuring thorough case processing, reporting, and periodic safety updates across diverse therapeutic areas, including oncology and rare diseases.

Our modern facilities in Navi Mumbai span 18,000 square feet, equipped with a 64-bed clinical pharmacology unit, an advanced bioanalytical lab, and robust IT infrastructure for secure data management. The facility is access-controlled and monitored via CCTV, ensuring the safety and confidentiality of all research processes. Additionally, we partner with leading vendors and service providers to deliver seamless support in areas such as emergency healthcare, pathology, pest control, and translation services.

Driven by our vision to be a strategic and trusted partner, Vivotech Research Lab Pvt. Ltd. strives to deliver innovative, efficient, and ethically sound research solutions, building lasting partnerships that advance healthcare and benefit the global medical community.
